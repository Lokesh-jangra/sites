The Tech Founder's Exit

Entrepreneur

Challenge

After selling a SaaS company for $45M, the founder needed to transition from concentrated business wealth to a diversified, tax-efficient personal portfolio while planning for their family's multi-generational future.

Our Approach

We coordinated tax-efficient structuring of the exit proceeds, established a family trust, built a diversified investment portfolio, and created a philanthropic giving strategy — all within 90 days of closing.

Tax savings of $2.8M Family trust established Diversified portfolio deployed Charitable foundation created

The International Family

Globally Mobile

Challenge

A family with residences in London, Dubai, and New York needed coordinated wealth management across 3 jurisdictions with different tax regimes, reporting requirements, and regulatory frameworks.

Our Approach

We built a unified wealth dashboard, coordinated with local tax advisors in each jurisdiction, established compliant structures, and ensured seamless reporting across all entities.

Single consolidated view Compliant in all jurisdictions Optimised cross-border tax Simplified reporting

The Foundation Endowment

Institutional

Challenge

A mid-sized foundation with a $120M endowment needed to improve its investment governance, increase its distribution rate, and align its portfolio with its mission-driven values.

Our Approach

We restructured the investment policy, introduced ESG-aligned strategies, and built a portfolio that increased the annual distribution from 4% to 5.2% while maintaining long-term capital preservation.

Distribution increased 30% ESG alignment achieved Governance modernised Long-term growth maintained
