About Your Consultation

Everything you need to know before scheduling your first meeting with our advisory team.

Absolutely. Our initial consultation is complimentary and carries no obligation. We believe that a meaningful advisory relationship must begin with mutual understanding. This first conversation allows us to learn about your goals, and for you to evaluate whether our approach aligns with your needs. There is never any pressure to proceed.
Most initial consultations last between 30 and 60 minutes. This gives us ample time to discuss your current financial situation, understand your aspirations, and outline how our services might benefit you — without feeling rushed. We respect your time and will always stay focused on what matters most to you.
There is no formal preparation required. However, it can be helpful to have a general sense of your current financial picture — including approximate asset levels, any existing investment accounts, and your primary financial goals or concerns. If you have specific questions or documents you'd like to discuss, feel free to bring those along as well.
After our initial meeting, if there is a mutual fit, we will prepare a personalised proposal outlining our recommended approach, fee structure, and next steps. You are welcome to take as much time as you need to review it. If you decide to move forward, we begin with a comprehensive discovery process to build a strategy tailored precisely to your situation.
